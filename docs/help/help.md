# I Need Help!

**Stuck? That is totally normal, and you have three good options.** Search first, ask in Slack second, and open a support ticket for anything private. This page shows you which one fits your problem so you get unblocked fast.

Quick pick

Code or build problem? Search, then Slack. Account, gold, or shop problem that you would rather keep private? Open a support ticket with the help button. Either way, you are not stuck for long.

## Step 1: Search it up

Before asking anyone, try searching your question. Google, YouTube, Reddit, Stack Overflow, whatever fits. Most problems have already been solved by someone, and finding the answer yourself is a skill you will use forever.

Try to be **specific**. "KiCad footprint wrong size" beats "PCB help." Include the exact error message if you have one. The more precise your search, the faster you find the fix.

## Step 2: Ask on Slack

If searching did not work, ask in one of these channels on the [Hack Club Slack](https://slack.hackclub.com). Pick the one that matches your question:

| Channel             | Use it for                                                                |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **#macondo**        | General questions about the program, projects, or how things work.        |
| **#macondo-help**   | When you are stuck on something specific and need someone to take a look. |
| **#hackatime-help** | Hackatime setup or time tracking issues.                                  |

When you ask, give people enough to help you in one reply. Include:

* What you are **trying to do**.
* What you have **already tried**.
* Any **error messages** you are seeing.

Screenshots help a lot. A clear question usually gets a faster, better answer.

## Step 3: Open a support ticket

For account issues, funding or gold questions, or anything private that does not fit in a public channel, open a **support ticket**. This goes straight to the Macondo team, not the whole Slack.

Click the round **help button** in the bottom-right corner of any docs page. A short form opens: pick a category and priority, write a subject and the details, attach a screenshot if it helps, and send. We will reply as fast as we can, and you can track your reply right inside Macondo.

If you would rather use email, write to [macondo@hackclub.com](mailto:macondo@hackclub.com) instead. Same team, same inbox.

Private stuff goes here, not Slack

Anything about your account, your payout, an order, or a problem you do not want to post publicly belongs in a support ticket. Use the help button or email, not a public channel.
